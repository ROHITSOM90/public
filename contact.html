Frequently Asked Questions

Are your tools really free?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use with no hidden charges. We support the site through advertising and partnerships.

Is my data secure when using your tools?

Absolutely. Most tools process files directly in your browser without uploading to our servers. For tools that require server processing, files are deleted immediately after processing.

We'd love to hear your suggestions! Use the contact form or email us with your ideas for new tools.
